KANNUR

Kannur is famous for its beaches, lush green forests, and temples. The place is often termed as one of the perfect places for a vacation. Also, Kannur is known for its culture and history. Kannur has a historical significance as it was the part of Chera dynasty that ruled Kerala.

Tourism Places in Kannur

St. Angelo Fort

St. Angelo's Fort, popularly known as the Kannur Fort, is a massive triangular laterite fort and is among the most historic sites in Kannur. Constructed by the first Portuguese Viceroy, Don Francesco de Almeida in 1505, the fort is flanked by gigantic bastions that make for an imposing sight.

Arakkal Museum

The Arakkal museum is located close to the heart of Kannur city, and is a place that mist be visited to understand the history behind the Arakkal Ali Raja family, the only Muslim royal family in Kerala.

Thalassery Fort

Thalassery fort at Kannur established by the East India Company is a historical monument which stands as testimonial of colonial rule in Kerala.

Mapila Bay

The natural harbour of Mappila Bay is a water inlet great for boat rides.Mappila bay is a sightseeing spot in its own right, a lot need to be done to bring it at par with the best of local tourist spots.

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary

Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is also famous for the vast amount of butterfly species found here and during December you can see butterfly migration here. Most of these are endemic to the Western Ghats. The forest is peaceful.

Baby Beach

It is nicknamed Baby Beach as the water is so calm it is safe for very small children. Because of the calm water, snorkeling at this beach is safe for children and others new to snorkeling. The beach is popular mostly due to the lack of waves and the shallow water.
