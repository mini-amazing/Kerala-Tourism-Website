KASARAGOD

Kasaragod has famous tourist centres, including the international fame BEKAL FORT & BEACH. There are also places which can be converted to attract the tourists. Kasaragod is known as the Land of Gods, Forts, Rivers, Beautiful hills and lengthy sandy beaches.

Tourism Places in Kasaragod

Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort is a medieval fort built by Shivappa Nayaka of Keladi in 1650 AD, at Bekal.

Bekal Beach

Bekal beach is popular for its backwaters, forts and scenic beauty.Situated on the broad expanse of the Malabar coastline.

Malik Deenar Juma Masjid

The mosque, Juma Masjid, which is one of the best kept and most attractive in the district, is located at Thalankara.

Ranipuram Hills

Dubbed as the "Ooty of Kerala", Ranipuram hills is renowned for its cool climate, deep woods and enthralling trekking trails

Chandragiri Fort

Chandragiri in Kasaragod,is unique in its history, antiquity and cultural and ecological geography. The Chandragiri Fort is situated at the banks of the Chandragiri River flowing through the blessed hills of Chandragiri.
